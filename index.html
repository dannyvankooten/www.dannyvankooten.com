Hi. I'm Danny, a nerd in his early thirties.

I've been selling software as my main and only source of income for well over 10 years now. According to some, I've never had a "real" job. But I know that my work is as real as it gets.

It all started out with me being hospitalized in Vietnam due to acute appendicitis and thus having some computer time. I used that time to build Mailchimp for WordPress, which is now in active use on well over 2 million websites out there.

Some more software products followed while I worked from Asia for the next few years. Nowadays, I am usually working from home in The Netherlands while trying to be a father for my 2 daughters.
